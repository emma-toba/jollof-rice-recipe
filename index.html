Jollof Rice

A vibrant, one-pot West African dish of rice cooked in a flavorful, spicy, and savory tomato-based broth with onions, peppers, and other spices.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time:49 minutes
  • Servings: 6
  • Difficulty: Easy

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 5 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers and onions.
  2. Heat oil and fry the mixture for 10 minutes.
  3. Add tomato paste and stir well.
  4. Season with curry, thyme, salt and cubes.
  5. Add the rice and water, then cover to cook.
  6. Simmer until rice absorbs the sauce completely.

Tips

Use foil to trap the steam for softer grains.

Smoky flavour? Cook on slightly high heat at the end.
